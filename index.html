Welcome to Harley Farms

Harley Farms is a farmstead dairy. We make cheese with the milk of our 200 alpine goats on nine acres of pasture in Pescadero, California. The farm grew from six pet goats to today's herd, with their delicious, critically-acclaimed cheese. The farm includes neighboring fields, a historic barn, an orchard and a secluded garden.

Open Thursday-Monday 11 am-4 pm, March through December.
