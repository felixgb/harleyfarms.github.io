Harley farms

The Harley Farms team includes three cheesemakers, a goatherd, a farm manager and a farm cook. Our mission is to provide food and an experience that is truly sustainable and local for our community and visitors. We pride ourselves on the integrity of our water use, diversification, and good citizenry. Harley Farms is open year-round for farm and dairy tours, retreats, and events in our atmospheric hayloft. Tour visitors see a working farm, and watch the milk move from goat to dairy, from curd to cheese. The farm is open during the winter months from 11am to 3pm Monday–Thursday, and from 10am to 4 pm Friday–Sunday, for unscheduled visits.

Where to buy

What can I do if I'm not with a tour group?

Visitors may stroll into the farm to see goats and llamas in paddocks and pastures. In the spring, there are often lively babies in Tony's Pen. Don't miss the hayloft with our magnificent fir table and carved chairs, where we host parties and farm dinners. The Cheese Shop at the dairy, open from 10 to 5, features our award-winning ricotta, feta, chevre and fromage blanc cheeses, plus a luxurious range of goat milk lotions and soaps, jellies and honey.
