I wish I could take more time out of the day to just stop and smell the roses. Those moments for yourself are precious, and I don't seem to fit them into the day as much as I'd like. When I was growing up in Yorkshire, the powerful throat lozenges known as Fishermen's Friends were extremely popular for sore throats.
