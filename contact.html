Harley Farms Goat Dairy

Come into life on our farm. Join our days as we move through the seasons. Welcome the babies in Spring, taste the sweet milk of Summer, smell the scents of Autumn and feel the warmth and quiet of Winter. The farm never stops.
The people and animals who live and work here reflect the dignity of farm life. Our land, buildings and water are cared for and appreciated. Our community is important and our personal lives matter.
We work with the farm and the farm works with us. We are family.
